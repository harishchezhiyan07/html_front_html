TN Result 2026

The 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election gave a surprising result, as Vijay’s political party, TVK, became the single largest party by winning 108 seats. However, no party reached the majority mark of 118 seats, leading to a hung assembly in the state. The DMK won 59 seats, while AIADMK secured 47 seats. Smaller parties like Congress, PMK, and IUML also won a few seats. After the results, political parties started discussions and alliance talks to form the new government in Tamil Nadu.Elections to appoint the 234 members of the 17th Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, the highest body of the Government of Tamil Nadu, were held on 23 April 2026. The results were declared on 4 May 2026 by the Election Commission of India. It recorded the highest voter turnout in the state's history (85.1%).
