CHENNAI SUPER KINGS

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are one of the most successful franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL), winning the tournament five times (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, and 2023). Captained by Ruturaj Gaikwad and coached by Stephen Fleming, they play their home matches at the M.A. Chidambaram Stadium (Chepauk) in Chennai, India.
